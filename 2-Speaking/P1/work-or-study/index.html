    Work or Study

    In IELTS Speaking Part 1, this is a very common opening question, and examiners expect a clear, natural, present-tense answer. Here are safe, high-scoring templates you can adapt.

    1️⃣ If you are a student (most common)

    Simple & natural

    I’m currently a university student. I’m majoring in computer science.

    • a X student
      • university
      • full-time
    • major in
    • computer science

    Slightly expanded (Band 7+)

    I’m currently studying computer science at university. I’m in my final year, so I’m quite busy with coursework and projects.

    • at university
    • be in my X year
      • first
      • second
      • final

    If you want to sound more fluent

    At the moment, I’m a full-time student, majoring in computer science. I’m especially interested in operating systems and software development.

    • be interested in
    • OS: operating systems

    2️⃣ If you are working

    Simple

    I’m currently working as a software developer.

    • currently
    • work as

    Expanded

    I work as a software developer for a small tech company, and I mainly focus on backend development.

    • tech company

    3️⃣ If you are BOTH studying and working (very common)

    I’m currently a full-time student, but I also do some part-time work alongside my studies.

    • do X work

    Or:

    I’m studying at university, and at the same time I’m doing a part-time job to gain some practical experience.

    • do X job

    4️⃣ If you are not working or studying right now (be honest, but positive)

    At the moment, I’m not working or studying. I’ve just finished my degree, and I’m preparing for the IELTS exam and looking for job opportunities.

    This is completely acceptable in IELTS.

    ⚠️ Common mistakes to avoid

    ❌ I study computer science now
    ❌ I am learning in university
    ❌ Very long life stories

    ✔ Use present continuous or present simple
    ✔ 1–2 sentences is perfect
    ✔ Sound relaxed, not memorized

    🎯 Examiner’s expectation (important)

    This question is:

    • NOT a test of ideas

    • Just a warm-up

    • Mainly checking your fluency and tense accuracy

    ✅ IELTS Speaking Part 1 推荐答案（学生版，Band 7+）

    I’m currently a university student.
    I’m in my final year, majoring in computer science, and at the moment I’m preparing to apply for postgraduate study overseas.

    如果你想稍微多说一句（但仍然安全）：

    I’m currently a university student.
    I’m in my final year, majoring in computer science, and I’m preparing my applications to study abroad after graduation.

    🗣️ 如果考官追问（很常见）

    Q: Why did you choose this subject?

    I chose computer science because I enjoy problem-solving and building practical systems, and I’d like to work in the tech industry in the future.

    Q: What are you planning to do after graduation?

    I’m planning to study abroad for my master’s degree, preferably in a computer-related field.

    ⚠️ 一个重要提醒（很多中国考生会犯）

    不要说：

    • ❌ I am learning computer science

    • ❌ I study in Hefei University now

    • ❌ My university was renamed before

    要说：

    • ✔ I’m majoring in…

    • ✔ I’m currently a student…

    • ✔ I’m in my final year…

    你的身份设定（统一口径）
    Final-year university student, majoring in computer science, preparing for postgraduate study abroad.

    🔹 Topic 1: Work or Study

    1️⃣ Do you work or study?

    I’m currently a university student.
    I’m in my final year, majoring in computer science.

    2️⃣ What do you study?

    I study computer science, which focuses on both software development and theoretical foundations.

    3️⃣ Why did you choose this subject?

    I chose computer science because I enjoy problem-solving and working with technology, and it offers strong career prospects.

    4️⃣ Is it a popular subject in your country?

    Yes, it’s quite popular, especially in recent years, because the tech industry is developing very quickly.

    5️⃣ What part of your studies do you like most?

    I really enjoy courses that involve practical projects, because I like applying theory to real-world problems.

    6️⃣ What part do you find most difficult?

    I find some theoretical courses challenging, especially those that involve abstract concepts and heavy mathematics.

    7️⃣ How much time do you spend studying?

    I spend several hours a day studying, especially now that I’m in my final year and preparing for future applications.

    8️⃣ Do you prefer studying alone or with others?

    I usually prefer studying alone, because it helps me concentrate, but I sometimes discuss difficult topics with classmates.

    9️⃣ What are you planning to do after graduation?

    I’m planning to apply for a master’s degree overseas and continue studying in a computer-related field.

    🔟 Do you think your subject will be useful in the future?

    Definitely. Computer science skills are highly transferable and useful in many industries.

    🔹 Topic 2: Daily Study Life (常见延伸)

    1️⃣ Do you enjoy being a student?

    Yes, I do. Although it can be stressful, I enjoy learning new things and developing my skills.

    2️⃣ What does a typical study day look like for you?

    I usually attend classes in the morning and study or work on assignments in the afternoon or evening.

    3️⃣ Do you think students today are under pressure?

    Yes, many students face pressure from exams, future careers, and competition, especially in their final year.

    4️⃣ Would you like to change your major?

    No, I wouldn’t. I think computer science suits my interests and long-term goals well.

    🔹 Topic 3: Work (如果考官硬切到 work)

    Have you ever worked?

    I’ve done some part-time or project-based work related to my studies, mainly to gain practical experience.

    （即使没正式工作，这样说也完全安全）

    🎯 使用说明（非常重要）

    • 不要一次说太多

    • 语速放慢一点

    • 每个答案说完就停，等考官

    记住一句话：

    Part 1 = 自然聊天，不是展示简历

    • I'm a student at X and I study X
    • I'm in my X year
      • first
      • second
      • final
    • I'll graduate
      • in summer this year
      • in two year's time
      • next year
    • I hope to graduate next year

    Do you work or study?

    I'm a student actually. I study CS (Computer Science) at university. I'm in my final year there, so yes, it's quite exiting.
